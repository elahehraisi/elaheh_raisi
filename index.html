Elaheh Raisi

Welcome! I'm Elaheh Raisi, an Applied Researcher at eBay working on Ad Tech and CPC ranking systems. I have a PhD in Computer Science/Machine Learning and previously worked as an ML Engineer at LinkedIn on recommendation systems.

My work spans machine learning, ranking systems, recommendation systems, and applied research.
